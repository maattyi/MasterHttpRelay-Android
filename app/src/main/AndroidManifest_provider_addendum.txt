NOTE: FileProvider registration for cert export.
This is appended into AndroidManifest.xml at the end of the <application> block.
The actual provider is declared inline below to keep this single file standalone:

<provider
    android:name="androidx.core.content.FileProvider"
    android:authorities="${applicationId}.fileprovider"
    android:exported="false"
    android:grantUriPermissions="true">
    <meta-data
        android:name="android.support.FILE_PROVIDER_PATHS"
        android:resource="@xml/file_paths" />
</provider>
